## Boss cadence

- **B1** after W1–W3: foundation of UX evidence, HCD and psychology.
- **B2** after W4–W6: user insight, concept design and task flow.
- **B3** is the Week 8 midterm boss: evidence-to-wireframe design defense.
- **B4** after W9–W11: design system, responsive design and visual accessibility.
- **B5** is the Week 15 final boss: high-fidelity prototype, evaluation/iteration and design defense.

## Replay and challenge rules

1. Each regular mission draws random casefiles and shuffles answer positions.
2. Correct answers require a linked **Reason Check** for high mastery; speed alone cannot earn 3 stars.
3. Recent case history is retained to avoid immediate repeats.
4. Boss gates mix transfer cases and a boss scenario, requiring learners to combine earlier weeks rather than recall one vocabulary item.
5. Results transfer the learner back to the relevant studio artifact: task, friction, impact, redesign and test evidence.
6. A pass is low-pressure at 2 stars; 3 stars require consistent reasoning and limited hint use.
