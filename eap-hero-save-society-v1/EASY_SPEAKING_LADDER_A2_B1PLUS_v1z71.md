# v1z71 — Easy Speaking Ladder A2–B1+

## Speaking difficulty is now truly level-specific

| AI level | Time | Oral task |
|---|---:|---|
| A2 Foundation | 12–18 sec | 2 short sentences: topic + one source action/detail |
| A2+ Bridge | 18–25 sec | topic + source detail + short closing |
| B1 Core | 30–40 sec | topic + detail + why useful + closing |
| B1+ Stretch | 45–60 sec | topic + evidence + why it matters + careful conclusion |

## Key A2 change
A2 does **not** require:
- signposting
- an evidence explanation
- a closing sentence
- typing a transcript

For S1 Weekly Goal Tracker, a complete A2 response can be:

> This source is about a weekly study goal.  
> The student summarizes one article on Tuesday and checks it on Friday.

## Scoring
Speaking remains oral-first:
- timer
- only the oral elements required at the learner's AI level
- transcript/notes are optional and cannot lower the mission score
