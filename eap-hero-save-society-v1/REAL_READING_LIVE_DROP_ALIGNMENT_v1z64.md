# v1z64 Real Reading Live Drop Alignment

## Critical correction
Before v1z64, Reading often showed only a one-sentence scenario, while its questions and sentence frames could come from a different mission.

## New Reading contract
Every Reading Mission contains:
1. A short 90–130 word Live Drop passage.
2. A clear source type and British co-teacher role.
3. Silent reading followed by one authentic human reading/clarification opportunity.
4. Three short evidence-based questions aligned to the exact passage.
5. Matching frames, vocabulary and short-answer placeholders.

## S1 Skill Diagnosis example
Students read a full student-support case about Narin:
- casual chat is not enough for academic communication;
- he struggles with summarising and evidence;
- his goal needs skill + weekly action + progress check.
